Welcome to GAT

Established in 2001, the Global Academy of Technology is spread over the 8-acre campus in Bangalore. It provides undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in the field of Engineering and Technology. It mainly offers these degrees in six core engineering subjects. The Global Academy of Technology - GAT - is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University. The college is known to provide excellent placements for the courses.
